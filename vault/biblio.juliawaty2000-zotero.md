## Abstract

The structure and absolute configuration of an Î±-pyrone isolated from Cryptocarya strictifolia was elucidated as 6R-(4'R,6'R-dihydroxy-8'-phenyloct-1'-enyl)-5,6-dihydro-2H-pyran-2-one. Pinocembrin and lysicamine were also isolated. (C) 2000 Elsevier Science Ltd.
